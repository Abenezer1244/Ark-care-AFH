Our Care Approach

24/7 Support

At Ark Care AFH, we believe in a person-centered care approach that respects individuality and promotes independence. Our services are designed around each resident's unique needs, preferences, and goals. We work closely with families, healthcare providers, and residents to develop comprehensive care plans that ensure the highest quality of life possible. We accept multiple programs including HCS , DDA Meaningful Day Services , Expanded Community Services , and Specialized Behavior Support .
