Our Care Approach

At Ark Care AFH, we believe in a person-centered care approach that respects individuality and promotes independence. Our services are designed around each resident's unique needs, preferences, and goals.

We work closely with families, healthcare providers, and residents to develop comprehensive care plans. We accept multiple programs including HCS, DDA Meaningful Day Services, and Specialized Behavior Support.

Each resident receives an individualized care plan developed in consultation with family members and healthcare providers.
