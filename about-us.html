About Ark Care

Driven by compassion,
defined by care.

We founded Ark Care with a simple promise: to treat every resident not just as a patient, but as a beloved family member deserving of dignity, joy, and comfort.

Our Mission

To provide a sanctuary where professional medical care meets the warmth of home. We strive to empower our residents, honoring their individual stories while ensuring their safety and well-being every single day.

We believe that care is not just about medical attention, but about nurturing the human spirit. Our holistic approach integrates physical health, emotional comfort, and social connection to create a truly supportive environment.

Our Vision

A future where assisted living celebrates life at every stage. We envision a community where age is not a limitation but a new chapter of connection, comfort, and dignified living.

We aim to redefine the standard of senior care by pioneering innovative technologies and sustainable practices that enhance quality of life. Our goal is to build a legacy where every resident feels valued, heard, and deeply at home.

History

Our Journey

From a small family initiative to a leading care provider.

The Foundation

2018

Ark Care was established by the Thompson family, converting their estate into a boutique care home with just 4 beds, driven by a personal need for better care options.

Expanding Horizons

2020

Amidst global challenges, we expanded our facility to accommodate 12 residents, introducing our signature 24/7 nursing rotation and smart health monitoring systems.

Community Excellence

2023

Recognized as the "Top Adult Family Home" in the region. We launched our community outreach program, integrating local youth and arts into our residents' daily lives.
