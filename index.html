FAQ

Common Questions

Quick answers to help you make the best decision for your loved one.

What is the admission process?

Our admission process begins with a free consultation and tour. Following this, our registered nurse performs a comprehensive care assessment to develop a personalized care plan. We guide you through paperwork and coordinate with medical providers for a seamless transition.

What services are included?

We provide all-inclusive care: 24/7 supervision, medication management, home-cooked meals, housekeeping, and laundry. Our team assists with all ADLs including bathing, dressing, and mobility, plus appointment scheduling and healthcare coordination.

Can family visit anytime?

Absolutely. Maintaining strong family connections is vital. You are welcome to visit at any time. We maintain an open-door policy while asking for courtesy during late-night hours to respect residents' rest.

Are you licensed?

Yes, Ark Care AFH is fully licensed by the Washington State DSHS. We comply with all state regulations. Our home and staff undergo regular inspections and background checks to ensure the highest safety standards.

What activities are offered?

We tailor engagement to abilities and interests. Our calendar includes music therapy, gentle exercise, crafts, gardening, games, and movie nights. We also celebrate special occasions together as a family.

How do we handle concerns?

We have a strict open-door policy. Families have direct access to our manager 24/7. We take all feedback seriously and work immediately to address concerns for your peace of mind.
